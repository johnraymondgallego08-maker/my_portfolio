## Focus

This activity focused on understanding the real problem behind the Workforce Accountability and Attendance System before moving deeper into implementation. The research looked at how manual attendance records, delayed reporting, and scattered files can affect payroll accuracy and workforce monitoring.

## Research Notes

- Defined the main problem around attendance manipulation, payroll miscalculation, no real-time monitoring, and department-based record storage.
- Created an HR officer persona to represent the person who manages attendance records, prepares payroll reports, and needs audit-ready information.
- Built an empathy map to understand the user's daily pressure, repeated disputes, manual checking, and need for a more transparent process.
- Used SCAMPER and brainstorming to explore secure attendance verification, QR code and photo capture, GPS validation, centralized records, and role-based access control.
- Connected the research output to the system direction used later for attendance monitoring, employee records, payroll support, and admin workflows.

## Outcome

The research clarified what the system needed to solve: accurate attendance validation, faster monitoring, cleaner payroll support, and a more trustworthy record process for both employees and administrators.
